About Us

What is Society260?

Society 260 stands as an independent communicative initiative driven by a resolute commitment to instigate actionable awareness surrounding mental health.

Society260 is a community.

At our carefully curated forums, we not only encourage individuals to show up authentically but also foster an inclusive environment where diverse experiences are respected. Beyond panel discussions, art therapy, and writing workshops, we offer a range of activations designed to empower individuals on transformative journeys of self-discovery and resilience, creating a dynamic space for collective growth.
